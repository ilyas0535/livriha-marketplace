Production Payment Gateway Setup Guide

Important: This guide is for setting up REAL payment processing. Only proceed if you have a legitimate business and proper authorization to receive payments.

Step 1: Create PayPal Business Account
  1. Go to PayPal Business
  2. Click "Get Started" and select "Business Account"
  3. Complete business verification with legal documents
  4. Link your business bank account
Step 2: Create Live Application
  1. Go to PayPal Developer
  2. Log in with your business account
  3. Click "Create App"
  4. Select "Live" environment (not Sandbox)
  5. Copy your Live Client ID and Secret
Requirements: Business verification, bank account linking, and compliance with PayPal's terms of service.

Step 1: Create Stripe Account
  1. Go to Stripe.com
  2. Click "Start now" and create account
  3. Complete business verification process
  4. Provide tax information and bank details
Step 2: Get Live API Keys
  1. Go to Dashboard → Developers → API Keys
  2. Toggle "View test data" OFF to see live keys
  3. Copy Publishable key (pk_live_...)
  4. Reveal and copy Secret key (sk_live_...)
Security: Never share your secret key. Store it securely and use HTTPS only.

Required Information
  • Bank Name: Full legal name of your bank
  • Account Holder: Your legal business name
  • Account Number: Your business account number
  • Routing Number: Bank's routing number
  • SWIFT Code: For international transfers
  • Bank Address: Physical address of bank branch
Legal Notice: Only provide account information for accounts you legally own and are authorized to receive business payments into.

Business Requirements
  • Valid business registration/license
  • Tax identification number
  • Business bank account
  • Compliance with local e-commerce laws
Platform Compliance
  • PayPal Acceptable Use Policy
  • Stripe Terms of Service
  • PCI DSS compliance for card processing
  • Data protection regulations (GDPR, etc.)
Recommendation: Consult with legal and financial advisors before processing real payments.
